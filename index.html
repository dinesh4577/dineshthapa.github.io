ABOUT US

Why we're different

Gorkha United Public School was established in 1999 AD (2057 B.S) as a private academic institute initiated and located in Kohalpur, Rajhena, Banke district of Nepal. It is affiliated to the National Examinations Board (NEB) and approved by Ministry of Education. It offers Ten Plus Two (10+2) programs under Management and Science streams. Gorkha United Public Secondary School, Kohalpur, Banke provides Montessori to Ten Plus Two level education with moderate and affordable fees structures. It also offers various scholarships facility for deserving, hard-working, brilliant, Dalit, Janajati, Madhesi, Aadivasi and deprived students. Gorkha United Public School provides various facilities such as Separate Girls and Boys hostels, Dance, Music, Cafeteria, Insurance, Sports, Library, Science Labs, Transpiration, Computer Labs, educational tours, multimedia, Journal, Parking, scholarship, club, counselling, and conference.
